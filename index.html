Bangladesh Launches New Satellite


Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has successfully launched its second satellite, marking a significant milestone in the country’s space program. The satellite, named Bangabandhu-2, will provide advanced communication services to rural areas and support disaster management efforts.

Nothing to be worried about HMPV virus: IEDCR


Date: 06/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

The Institute of Epidemiology, Disease Control and Research (IEDCR) has said that the people of the country have nothing to be worried about the risks of the Human Metapneumovirus (HMPV) at the moment. IEDCR Director Professor Tahmina Shirin told the news that HMPV was first detected in Bangladesh in 2017.

Earthquake jolts Dhaka, other parts of the country


Date: 07/01/2025 Time: 10.30 AM

An earthquake was felt in various parts of the country, including the capital Dhaka, on Tuesday morning. The Meteorological Department reported that the earthquake originated in China, with a magnitude of 7.1 on the Richter scale.
